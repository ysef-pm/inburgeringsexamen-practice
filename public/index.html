Exam Tips

  • Schrijven (40 min): 4 writing tasks - emails, forms, short texts
  • Lezen (65 min): 25 multiple choice questions based on texts
  • Write short, clear sentences - simple and correct is better than complex and wrong
  • Use signal words: eerst, daarna, dan, en, maar, omdat, want
  • For reading: read the question first, then find the answer in the text

Official Writing Rubric (5 Categories, 10 pts max)

  • Execution (0-3 pts): Did you address all parts of the task?
  • Grammar (0-2 pts): Correct verb placement and conjugation
  • Spelling (0-2 pts): Words spelled correctly
  • Clearness (0-1 pt): Structure with conjunctions (en, maar, want, omdat)
  • Vocabulary (0-2 pts): Varied word choice
